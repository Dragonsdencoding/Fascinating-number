Fascinating Number:When a 3 digit number(n) is concatinated with the multiple of 2 and 3(n*2,n*3)and we get
a number which contains all the digits 1 to 9
exactly once.Such a three digit number is known as fascinating number.

Note:There could be any no. of zeroes ,but they are ignored.But all the other digits from 1 to 9 must
occured only once.

for example:

273 is a fascinating number.

273*1=273
273*2=546
273*3=819

After concatinating all the numbers,we get the final number 273546819.
As you can see all the no.s from 1 to 9 occured once.
